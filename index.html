myGame of Life

This be my implementation of Conway's Game of Life, a project I'm embarking on in order to gain some experience working with HTML5, CSS3, and probably both JavaScript and Coffee Script.

The rules, more for my benefit than yours, are as follows:

  • Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbors dies.
  • Any live cell with more than three live neighbors dies
  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbors survives
  • Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbors becomes alive.
