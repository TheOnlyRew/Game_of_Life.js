Read the full description of Conway's Game of Life.

    The rules are as follows:
  • Every `generation` of the game, the cells are updated synchronously
  • Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbors dies
  • Any live cell with more than three live neighbors dies
  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbors survives
  • Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbors becomes alive
    The basic controls are:
  • Left-click any empty cell to 'raise' it–to make it alive
  • Left-click any live cell to kill it
  • Left-click and drag to either batch raise or batch kill cells
  • Right-click and drag anywhere on the board to move the view frustum
  • Hold both the Left and Right mouse buttons to zoom–up to zoom in, down to zoom out

The control pane to the right will allow you to play/pause the simulation, take a single step to the next generation, set the speed of the simulation, and completely reset the board.

All visible panes are movable, and you can hide this help pane by clicking the hide anchor to the right of the title text.
